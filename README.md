##  Task 1: Data Cleaning & Preparation  

**Objective:** Prepare high-quality, analysis-ready datasets.

**Actions Performed:**
- Removed duplicate records  
- Standardized state and gender values  
- Corrected invalid entries  
- Unified date formats  
- Handled missing and anomalous data  

---

##  Task 2: Customer Segmentation  

- Segmentation by Wealth Segment  
- Segmentation by Gender  
- Segmentation by Job Industry  

**Insight:** Certain wealth segments and job industries contribute significantly to long-term customer value.

---

##  Task 3: Transaction Analysis  

- Monthly sales trend analysis  
- Brand and product-line performance  
- Top 10 customers by transaction value  
- Average purchases per customer  

**Insight:** A small group of customers generates a large share of revenue.

---

##  Task 4: New Customer Insights  

- Wealth and industry distribution  
- Geographic concentration by state  
- Property valuation vs wealth correlation  
- Potential revenue estimation  

---

##  Task 6: Executive Summary & Recommendations
